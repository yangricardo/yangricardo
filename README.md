## Hi there, I’m Yang Ricardo 👋
### Tech Lead | Software Architect | Web3 & DLT Enterprise | Knowledge Graphs & Semantic Data

I am a Tech Lead, Software Architect, and R&D Researcher who bridges the gap between enterprise software engineering, decentralized ledgers (Blockchain), and semantic knowledge architectures. 

Currently, I am a Master's Candidate in Computer Science at **DI/PUC-Rio**, focusing my research on **Knowledge Graphs and Semantic Interoperability**.

Know more at: [yang.dev.br](https://yang.dev.br)

---

### 🔭 What I am working on:
- **Decision Support Systems (DSS):** Engineering high-performance architectures and Web Semantics for subsea engineering asset risk prediction in the Oil & Gas sector.
- **Web3 & Enterprise DLT:** Researching and developing private, permissioned, and hybrid blockchain solutions (Hyperledger Fabric, Hyperledger Besu, and the Brazilian Blockchain Network - RBB).
- **Data Re-engineering & Knowledge Exploration:** Conducting technical consultancy on legacy relational database migrations, reverse engineering, and semantic graph integration.
